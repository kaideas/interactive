CONCERT

4 PM

To celebrate this momentous year, California Summer Music will be holding the 20+80 event on Sunday, July 19th, consisting of a concert and a celebration dinner. The concert, at 4pm, will feature both student and faculty performers, and works by a variety of composers. The first half of the concert will represent the future, present, and legacy, while the second half will represent community. Dinner will follow immediately afterwards and will feature birthday cake and some of Sonoma County's famous wine!
