CONCERT

7 PM

To celebrate this momentous year, California Summer Music will be holding the 20+80 event on Sunday, July 19th, consisting of a concert and a reception. The concert, at 7pm, will feature both student and faculty performers, and works by a variety of composers. The first half of the concert will represent the future, present, and legacy, while the second half will represent community. The reception will follow immediately afterwards and will feature some of Sonoma County's famous wine, among other hors d'oeuvres and goodies!
